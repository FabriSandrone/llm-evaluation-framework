# Core Evaluation Metrics

## Instruction Compliance (IC)
Measures adherence to explicit structural and content constraints.

## Factual Accuracy (FA)
Measures alignment with accepted domain knowledge.

## Completeness (C)
Measures whether essential aspects are covered.

## Logical Consistency (LC)
Evaluates internal coherence.

## Clarity & Precision (CP)
Assesses interpretability and exactness of language.

## Risk
Estimates direct harm potential.

## Hidden Risk
Captures downstream amplification effects.
